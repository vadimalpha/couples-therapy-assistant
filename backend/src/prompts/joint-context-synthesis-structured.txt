- Primary vs. secondary emotions for each partner
- Attachment needs and styles
- The "demon dialogue" they're caught in

**Nonviolent Communication (NVC)**
- Observations vs. evaluations from each partner
- Underlying feelings and needs

- Empathic listening opportunities

3. **Attachment Dynamic Mapping**
   Identify each partner's attachment needs and how they're interacting:
   - What is each person seeking (security, closeness, autonomy)?
   - How do their attachment styles interact?
   - Where are the attachment injuries?

4. **Framework-Specific Guidance for This Partner**
   Apply relevant techniques personalized to their position in the cycle:
   - EFT interventions for accessing primary emotions
   - Gottman techniques for breaking the cycle
   - NVC approaches for needs-based communication

5. **Skill-Building and Homework**
