## Key Takeaways for Terminus
1. **Flexible Views**: Offer multiple ways to view connections (list, grid, grouped)
2. **Custom Properties**: Let users add custom fields to contacts (interests, last met, notes)
3. **Smart Filters**: Save views like "Haven't contacted in 30 days" or "Share interest in hiking"
4. **Templates**: Provide templates for different relationship types
5. **Quick Add**: Floating button or quick capture for new connections/activities
6. **Tags System**: Multi-select tags for interests and relationship types
7. **Search Everything**: Powerful search across all data
8. **Relationship Links**: Connect contacts to shared activities or events
